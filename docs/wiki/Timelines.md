# Timelines

Tribe offers several timelines so you can see different slices of the fediverse.

## Home

Posts from people you follow, in chronological order. No algorithm. Just the people you chose.

## Local

Public posts from everyone on **gettribe.org** (your neighbours on this server). A good way to discover who's on Tribe.

## Social

Home + Local combined — your follows, plus people on gettribe.org you haven't followed yet.

## Global (Federated)

Public posts from every server Tribe knows about. This is the whole fediverse firehose — fast-moving, noisy, but the best place to discover new people and communities.

## Lists and Antennas

- **Lists**: manually curated groups of accounts you want to read together (like Twitter lists).
- **Antennas**: filter the firehose by keyword, domain, or user — great for following topics without following every account.

Find them in the sidebar → *Lists* and *Antennas*.
